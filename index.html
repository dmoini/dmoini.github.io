About Me

I am currently an undergraduate student at Loyola Marymount University pursuing my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science with a minor in Philosophy. Throughout my time in college, I have been involved with the Residence Hall Association, National Residence Hall Honorary, MAGIS Service Organizaiton, an Orientation leader, and more.

Some fun facts about me include I was born and raised in the wonderful city of Los Angeles my entire life (and I do not plan on leaving anytime soon), I am a soccer fan and support Chelsea FC, I love watching movies and television shows, especially Brooklyn Nine-Nine, Rick and Morty, and Black Mirror, and I love meeting new people and spending time with my friends.

I have enjoyed computer science and programming so much since I first started. Funny enough, it was a last minute change before I came to college to become a computer science major. Luckily enough, I have been able to pursue a field that both intrigues and challenges me on a daily basis, and I am excited to see how much more I will grow within this field.

Additionally, I am incredible passionate about service and I have been doing service throughout all of college, and I plan on continuing to do so beyond college. I hope that with this passion and my evergrowing knowledge and skills in computer science, I can make a positive impact around me.

Playroll

Playroll is an application that allows users to create playrolls, which replace the traditional idea of playlists. Using a set of unique algorithms, Playroll uses your provided music to create the perfect playlist. Worked on backend using Go, GORM, GraphQL, and PostgresDB.

Casper Programming Language

Collaborated with four other LMU students to create Casper: The Friendly Programming Language. Casper incorporates a speech-like, easy-to-use syntax that eliminates unnecessary verboseness from the programming experience. Casper is reminiscent of Python with nuanced features and syntax designed to make its meaning as transparent as possible and make programming an entirely pleasant affair.

Fortnite Stat Tracker

Fortnite web application that retrieves player stats, daily shop items, and weekly challenges. Coded with HTML, JavaScript/jQuery, CSS, Bootstrap, and Fortnite API.

Hog

Created a system utility function that outputs which process IDs are using up the highest CPU percentage on your machine.
